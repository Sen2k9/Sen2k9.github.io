Lessons

An holistic approach to music lessons that guides the student toward
individual creative expression in melody, rhythm, and harmony.

Classes are offered both online, and in-person from my studio in Portland, OR.

Offerings:

Violin, Viola, Composition, Classical Indian Music, Improvisation, Music Production & Engineering, Ableton

I began teaching music while studying Composition at Berklee College of Music over 15 years
ago. My style and format has changed through the years. What I work with students on at the
core is finding what makes the music sing within them, and bringing it out. There are many
techniques that I use, and they vary depending on the student and the goals. The opportunity
to share my passion for this craft is a huge gift and continues to cultivate lots of joy.

The Fine Print

I offer one introductory lesson, after which, if we decide to continue, I ask for a month's commitment of weekly lessons. Beyond the first month, I operate on the academic calendar, with commitment to one full Trimester (Jan-May, June-Aug, Sep-Dec).

Payment:

All classes are one hour long, except for the introductory lesson, which is 40 minutes.
Scholarships for young students are available- just ask!

Cancellation:

More than 24 hours:
Please reschedule the lesson within the same calendar month billing cycle.

Less than 24 hours:
Please message me with possible reschedule times the same week. Otherwise, there is no refund for the canceled lesson.
